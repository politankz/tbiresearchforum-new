4. Presenting 2:00 pm - 2:30 pm

Oanh N Mandal, MD

Clinical Perspective on TBI in Aging Veterans

Dr. Mandal joined the VA Palo Alto in 2009 and is the lead Polytrauma Network Site physiatrist at the Livermore division. She works with TBI patients, ranging from mild to severe, OEF/OIF as well as Vietnam/Korean/Gulf war veterans. She graduated from Tufts University School of Medicine in Boston and completed her training in Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation at the University of California in Los Angeles (UCLA).
